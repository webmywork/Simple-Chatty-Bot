Counting positive numbers Open on JetBrains Academy

Write a program that reads a sequence and prints the amount of positive numbers in the sequence.

The first number is the length of the sequence. Other numbers are parts of this sequence.


Sample Input:
8
2 3 0 7 4 -2 -3 0

Sample Output:
4



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
